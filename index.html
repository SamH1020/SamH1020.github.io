Sailing Facts

Different and interesting facts on sailing.

  • The phrase "feeling blue" origanated from sailors, when a ship lost a captian the ship would fly blue flags to indicate their loss.
  • Sailing around the world westward is more difficult than sailing eastward, this is due to winds that push eastward being stronger and more predictable in the southern hemisphere.

What to bring

One of the most important things to sailing is too know what to expect, in terms of weather, tides, and wind. Here are some of the things you should bring with you when you go sailing (for a day):

  1. Bring a life jacket
  2. Pack sunscreen and bug spray, depending on where you are going
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Wear clothing that you don't mind if it gets wet
