What to bring

One of the most important things to sailing is too know what to expect, in terms of weather, tides, and wind. Here are some of the things you should bring with you when you go sailing:

  1. Bring a life jacket
  2. Pack sunscreen and bug spray, depending on where you are going
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Wear clothing that you don't mind if it gets wet

Sailing History

The history of sailing goes back thousands of years and taking root in almost every culture close to the sea.

  • The earliest depiction of a sailboat dates back too between 5,000 - 5,500 BC
  • Sailing has been used in some of the greatest expeditions in all of known history
  • Sailing first appeared at the Olympics, in 1900.
