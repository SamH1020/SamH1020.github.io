Sailing Facts

Different and interesting facts on sailing, (for reference 1 knot is equal too 1.15 mph)

  • The fastest speed of the average cruising sailboats is between 4-6 knots, (or 4.5-7 mph) and a top speed of 7 knots (8 mph).
  • The average speed of racing sailboats is 15 knots.
  • Several factors go into factoring the speed of a sailboat, such as wind speed, wieght of the boat, size and length.
  • The shape of the Hull can also determine the speed, handling, and the ships ability to take strong currents and large waves.

Sailing History

The history of sailing goes back thousands of years and taking root in almost every culture close to the sea.

  • The earliest depiction of a sailboat dates back too between 5,000 - 5,500 BC
  • Sailing has been used in some of the greatest expeditions in all of known history
  • Sailing first appeared at the Olympics, in 1900.
